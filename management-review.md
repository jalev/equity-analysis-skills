> **Format (follow AGENTS.md rules):** Write narrative paragraphs — not bullet lists. Use tables for guidance vs. actuals comparisons. Cite as `[FY20XX 10-K, Item 10]` or `[Proxy, Compensation Discussion]` — never line numbers. The `→` after a quote must add analytical interpretation, not paraphrase the quote.

Evaluate management quality and alignment using proxy statements, annual reports,
earnings transcripts, and any available filings.

Cover:

1. **Capital allocation track record** - historical ROE, ROIC vs WACC;
   quality of M&A (write-downs?), buyback timing (repurchased at what valuations?)
2. **Insider ownership** - CEO/CFO/board holdings as % of net worth (if estimable)
   and recent insider transactions
3. **Compensation structure** - what metrics drive bonuses? Are incentives aligned
   with long-term shareholder value or short-term EPS targets?
4. **Guidance credibility** - compare past guidance to actual results across
   multiple quarters; pattern of sandbagging, missing, or hitting?
5. **Communication quality** - are earnings calls forthright about problems
   or do they deflect? Do they acknowledge mistakes?
6. **Tenure and succession** - how long has current leadership been in place?
   Any succession risk?

Rate overall management quality: Excellent / Good / Adequate / Concerning
