> **Format (follow AGENTS.md rules):** Write narrative paragraphs — not bullet lists. Use a summary table for moat ratings. Cite as `[FY20XX 10-K, Item 1]` — never line numbers. The `→` after a quote must add analytical interpretation, not paraphrase the quote.

Assess this company's competitive advantages (moat) using evidence from the uploaded documents.

Evaluate each moat source - rate as Strong / Moderate / Weak / Absent:

1. **Switching costs** - what makes it painful for customers to leave?
   Evidence from retention rates, contract structures, integration depth.
2. **Network effects** - does the product become more valuable with more users?
   Evidence from user growth vs engagement metrics.
3. **Cost advantages** - structural cost edge from scale, process, or geography?
   Evidence from margin comparisons vs competitors.
4. **Intangible assets** - brands, patents, licenses, regulatory approvals?
   Evidence from pricing power, patent portfolio, regulatory barriers.
5. **Efficient scale** - does the company serve a market too small for new entrants?
   Evidence from market share stability, competitor entry/exit history.

Then assess **moat trajectory**: Widening / Stable / Narrowing - with evidence.

End with: "Moat summary" - 3 sentences on the overall defensibility of the business.
